EVENTS

Friq connects marginalized communities and groups with events and projects that are aimed at helping them. We partner with several NGOs to ensure that these projects and events are accesible and benefit as many people as possible.

Incase you are interested in an event we will redirect you to our partners who are hosting the event for more information.
