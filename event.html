Expanding Access to Secondary School Education for
Teenage Mothers in Kenya

In
20
days

Event Overview

Over a third of teenage mothers and their household heads were not aware of the provisions of the National School Health Policy (which permits pregnant learners to remain in school). An even larger proportion (nearly half) of both groups were not aware of the Free Secondary Education policy. A third of school principals were not aware of the National School Health policy, and awareness of their designated roles under the school re-entry policy was low.
